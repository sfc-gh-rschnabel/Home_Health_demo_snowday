# Sales Territory Assignment and Performance Policy

## Policy Number: SLS-001
## Department: Sales / Business Development
## Effective Date: January 1, 2026
## Last Reviewed: February 15, 2026

## Purpose
To establish fair and transparent territory assignment, quota methodology, and performance expectations for Lincare's 150+ sales representatives who market to physicians, discharge planners, and healthcare facilities.

## Territory Structure

### Geographic Hierarchy
- Region (7 total): Northeast, Southeast, Midwest, Southwest, West, Mid-Atlantic, Pacific
- Division: 2-4 per region based on population density
- Territory: Assigned to individual rep (typically covers 1-3 counties or metro area)

### Territory Assignment Criteria
Territories are balanced based on:
1. Total Addressable Market (TAM): Number of respiratory/DME Medicare beneficiaries in area
2. Existing Lincare presence: Number of active referral sources
3. Competitive density: Number of competing DME suppliers
4. Drive time: Maximum 90-minute radius from home base
5. Account concentration: No territory should have >40% of revenue from a single account

### Territory Sizing Targets
- Standard territory: 150-300 potential referral sources (physicians + facilities)
- Active coverage expectation: 80-100 accounts receiving regular contact (quarterly minimum)
- High-value accounts: Top 20% of referral volume require monthly face-to-face contact

## Quota Methodology

### Annual Quota Components
1. New referral quota: Number of new physician relationships generating referrals (target: 15-25/year)
2. Revenue quota: Total equipment revenue from territory (based on market potential)
3. Activity quota: Minimum monthly activities to maintain territory coverage

### Monthly Activity Minimums
- Face-to-face office visits: 60 per month
- Phone contacts: 80 per month
- Lunch/educational meetings: 4 per month
- In-service presentations: 2 per month
- New physician prospecting calls: 20 per month

### Quota Adjustment Factors
- New territory (first 6 months): 60% of standard quota
- Territory with departing rep (transition): 80% for 3 months
- Market growth adjustment: Annual review based on CMS utilization data
- Seasonal adjustment: Q1 typically 15% higher (insurance resets, new prescriptions)

## Performance Metrics and Compensation

### Key Performance Indicators
1. Lead-to-Referral Conversion Rate: % of physician contacts resulting in referrals (target: 12-18%)
2. Referral Source Value: Average monthly revenue per active referral source
3. New Physician Acquisitions: Net new referring providers per quarter
4. Market Penetration Rate: Lincare referrals vs. total CMS respiratory claims in territory
5. Activity Completion Rate: Actual vs. planned activities
6. Cost per Acquisition: Total sales cost / new referring provider acquired

### Performance Tiers
- President's Club (Top 10%): 120%+ of quota, premium territory assignment rights
- Above Target (Top 25%): 100-119% of quota
- At Target (50th-75th percentile): 85-99% of quota
- Below Target (Below 50th): 70-84% of quota, performance improvement plan initiated
- Critical (Bottom 10%): Below 70%, 90-day remediation plan

## CMS Market Data Utilization
Sales managers use CMS Medicare utilization data to:
- Identify high-volume respiratory prescribers not currently referring to Lincare
- Measure true market penetration against total available referrals
- Prioritize territories for expansion or additional rep coverage
- Validate quota reasonableness based on actual market size
- Identify emerging physician practices (new Medicare enrollees with DME referral patterns)

Data sources available via Snowflake Marketplace:
- CMS Provider Utilization and Payment Data
- Medicare Physician Compare
- DMEPOS Referring/Ordering Data
- Geographic Variation in Medicare Spending

## Territory Reassignment Triggers
- Rep departure: Territory reassigned within 5 business days to prevent referral source attrition
- Consistent underperformance: After 6 months below target with coaching, territory may be split
- Market growth: Territory split when TAM exceeds 2x standard territory size
- Acquisition: New locations merged into existing territory structure within 90 days
